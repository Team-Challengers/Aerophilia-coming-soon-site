ABOUT

Aerophilia is a National level Techno-Cultural fest, conducted for students who are aspiring to be extraordinary. It’s a 3-day long event spanning throughout the weekend with an exciting competition that involves RC Bot events, a race course by the Indian Drone Racing League and all things outta this world! There's also fun-filled activities such as a Musical concert, Hackathon by ISRO, and many more.
The motive behind organizing such an event is to spread and teach the young talented minds to explore new areas of technology and aeromodelling to foster the development of the nation.
